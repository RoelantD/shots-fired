The Format

TECH. TALK.
NO FILTER.

Each episode, both hosts arrive prepared. Both brings a piece of technology worth exploring, a news story worth dissecting, or some other topic worth debating.

No scripts. No guardrails. Just two people who care deeply about technology, sharing genuine takes — and pushing back on each other's.

Whether it's a breakthrough model, a controversial product launch, or a trend nobody is talking about — we cover it with depth, curiosity, and just enough snark.
